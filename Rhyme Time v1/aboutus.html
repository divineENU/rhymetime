About Us

Rhyme Time is a collaborative project created by Elisa, Divine, and Conor, designed to make learning nursery rhymes fun and interactive for children aged 3 to 6. Our goal is to support early literacy and cognitive development by combining karaoke-style lyrics, educational mini-games, and personalization features. Inspired by leading educational platforms, we believe learning should be enjoyable and accessible for every child. Our website is built with accessibility in mind, using clear layouts, large buttons, and engaging activities to ensure all children can join in. We are passionate about creating a safe and inspiring space for young learners to sing, play, and grow together.
