Our Project: Rhyme Time

Rhyme Time is a collaborative website designed to help children aged 3–6 learn classic nursery rhymes in a fun, interactive, and accessible way. Our site combines karaoke-style lyrics, instrumental audio, and educational mini games to promote literacy and cognitive development.

Our Goals

  • Make learning nursery rhymes accessible, fun, and engaging for all children.
  • Support early literacy and cognitive skills through music, repetition, and play.
  • Personalize the experience with user names and progress tracking.
  • Ensure accessibility for all users, including those with sensory sensitivities.

Key Features

  • Karaoke-style lyrics with synchronized instrumentals.
  • Personalisation: users see their name throughout the site.
  • Interactive educational games and quizzes for each rhyme.
  • Progress tracking using localStorage—no login required.
  • Form submission for suggesting new rhymes.

How We Work

  • We use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build interactive, responsive pages.
  • Each rhyme page features karaoke-style lyrics synchronized with instrumental audio.
  • Mini games and quizzes reinforce learning and provide instant feedback.
  • We prioritize accessibility with high-contrast colors, large buttons, and ARIA labels.
  • Our team collaborates closely, sharing tasks and supporting each other's learning.

Accessibility Commitment

Rhyme Time is designed for all children, including those with sensory sensitivities. We use high-contrast colors, large fonts, and ARIA labels for screen readers. Reduced motion settings are available for users who prefer less animation. Our goal is to meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 standards for inclusivity.

Looking Ahead

  • Adding more rhymes and mini games.
  • Printable lyric sheets and certificates.
  • Customizable avatars and enhanced reward systems.
  • Potential for group participation features in the future.
